    UPDATE 29.12. 2022

    29.12.2022 by DIANA AKHMETOVA COMMENTS

    Even on New Year’s Eve we are in the process of developing and refining the functionality of the SFERA app and updates are already available.

    In v0.0.99 for Android, we have added the option of blocking the person on the phone to ASAP and implemented a new button to create a chat. Users whose Android has already been updated to version 13 can now easily give permission to download files from their phone. And finding the right chat or contact is easy by ID.

    The number of posts is now directly linked to your rating. Each user can post exactly as much content per week as their rating indicates. Fixed bugs in ASAP when creating a chat with a contact via tap on the “Invite to SFERA” button and when adding post content.

    iOS users can also block users in ASAP, and voice messages from Android now arrive without bugs. In addition to all of the above, many other bugs have been fixed, which you help us identify.

    Use SFERA, improve your rating and don’t forget to send us suggestions on how to do better the app.

    02.08.2022 BY DIANA AKHMETOVA

    One of the features of the SFERA digital ecosystem is the rating. Each user has a starting rating of 2.5. It is displayed in the main profile next to the user’s photo. The rating affects the user’s interaction with other users in the system and the promotion of the profile.

    The user changes the rating by performing any action in the system. Other users rate publications. The owner of the publication cannot evaluate its content. The starting value of each publication corresponds to the user’s rating at the time of publication.

    A low rating does not limit the user’s capabilities, but a high one provides additional ones. For example, higher-rated users gain more trust in the ecosystem. Their opinion will be given priority if the publication is assessed differently.

    The rating does not reflect the popularity of the user, photo or video. This indicator in the system is used to determine the reliability and usefulness of published information, reflecting the trust of users and the system to its owner.

    The following shall be taken into account when calculating the rating:

    • rating of the person who rated:
    • publication/user rating before evaluation;
    • publication/user rating after evaluation;
    • evaluation of the publication.

    The first users have an advantage in increasing the rating of their profile. To do this, you just need to actively use the application. In the future, when the number of users in SFERA will actively grow, it will be much more difficult to increase the rating. We recommend starting to raise your rating in the SFERA digital ecosystem right now.
