superapplication

All services are an integral part of SFERA and work as a single application.

At the moment, the services of the first stage of development are available in the system.

  • Main Profile

  • SFERA Feed

  • ASAP

  • MeetNet

  • Choose

  • Black Box

  • Videos

  • Job Lite

  • Find Friends

  • MAIN PROFILE

    Service (“page”), where the owner (user) places information about himself and his environment in photo and video format.

  • SFERA FEED

    SFERA content aggregator, which will form your news feed with developing content. The service does not decide for you what to show you. You’ll see all the content you’re subscribed to, plus the best in the system for a certain period of time.

  • ASAP

    A multifunctional messenger that incorporates the best features of existing messengers.

  • MEETNET

    MeetNet is a dating service that syncs with your personal profile. People are selected on the basis of more than 50 parameters.This approach will make for near-perfect matches in the future.

  • CHOOSE

    Don’t know which is better? Use this service to find out what your audience thinks. In the future, this will be an effective tool for businesses to conduct analytical surveys.

  • BLACK BOX

    Allows you to find new communication by geolocation and chat on any topic with any user. For those who lack communication and who have questions on difficult topics to discuss.

  • VIDEOS

    Video service, the main advantage of which, providing exclusively useful, developing, interesting content.

  • JOB LITE

    A very simple and straightforward sub-application to find jobs or employees, for simple professions that do not require a resume. Direct dialogue between the job seeker and the employer.

  • FIND FRIENDS

    The first service with the sole purpose of finding new friends in real (offline) life.
